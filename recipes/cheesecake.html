Cheesecake Recipe                                                                                                                                                          Home

  • 1 1/2 cups Graham Cracker Crumbs
  • 2 tablespoon Sugar + 1 tablespoon Brown Sugar
  • 7 tablespoon Melted Butter
  • 32 oz Cream Cheese softened
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 2/3 cup Sour Cream
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon Vanilla Extract
  • Pinch of Salt
  • 4 large Eggs

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (160°C).


  2. Mix graham cracker crumbs, sugars, and melted butter. Press into a 9” springform pan.


  3. Add eggs one at a time, mixing gently.


  4. Pour batter into crust and bake for 50–60 minutes until edges are set but center is slightly jiggly..


  5. Cool gradually, then refrigerate at least 6 hours before serving.
