Chocolate Pudding Recipe                                                                                                                                                          Home

  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 1/2 cup Baking Soda
  • 1/4 cup Cornstarch
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 4 cups Whole Milk
  • 2 tablespoons Butter
  • 2 teaspoons Vanilla Extract
  • Whipped cream or topping of choice

Instructions

  1. In a heavy saucepan, whisk together sugar, cocoa, cornstarch, and salt.


  2. Gradually add milk, whisking until smooth.


  3. Bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring constantly. Cook for 2 minutes.


  4. Remove from heat, stir in butter and vanilla until smooth.


  5. Pour into serving dishes, chill until set. Garnish with whipped cream or toppings.
